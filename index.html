What does Table for Two do?
One app. Two people. Zero arguments about where to go.
Table for Two learns what you both love — cuisine, vibe, budget — then uses AI to suggest dates you'll actually agree on. Then it books everything: the restaurant, the cab, the hotel. One less thing to stress about.
✦
AI that knows you both
We use AI to curate date ideas based on both your tastes at the same time — not just what one of you likes, but what you'll both genuinely enjoy together
◈
Book without leaving the app
We connect directly to OpenTable, Resy and other reservation services so you can find availability and book your table right here — no switching between apps
◫
Everything in one place
Every date you've planned, every reservation you've made — all in one view. We'll send you reminders before each one so you never miss a thing
◉
It remembers what you love
Set your cuisines, dietary needs, vibes and budget once — we remember it all and use it every time we suggest something new
How it works
Three steps. One perfect evening.
We handle all the logistics so you just have to show up and enjoy each other.
1
Tell us what you both love
Set your cuisines, vibes, budget and any dietary needs. Takes two minutes. We remember it forever.
2
We plan and book everything
AI matches a date to both your tastes and we confirm the restaurant, pre-order the cab and hold the hotel — all at once.
3
Just show up
We send you a reminder when it's time to leave. Your table is waiting, your cab is outside. All you need to do is enjoy it.
